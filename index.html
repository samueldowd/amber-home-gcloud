About Me

Passionate about empowering people to achieve their dreams, I am currently a full-time dental hygienist on an elite dental team in downtown Boston. I am committed to helping provide services to those who lack access to oral health care. I fulfill this mission by traveling and providing comprehensive dental hygiene services including cleanings, oral hygiene instruction, fluoride treatments, and nutritional counselling. Additionally, I provide office trainings to help practices improve their periodontal protocols. I am always seeking opportunities to work with children, assist in underserved communities, and speak at Universities.

Experience

Education

I graduated in December 2010 from the University of New Haven, ahead of my program’s schedule, with a Bachelors of Science in Dental Hygiene. I am now working towards my Master's in Public Health at the University of New England. 

On the Job

I have experience in Pediatric dentistry as well as general and multi-specialty dental practices. While studying at the University of New Haven, I held a faculty position in which I taught clinical dental hygiene to junior level students. I’ve also traveled to Romania and Kenya to provide dental care to underserved communities.

Consulting/Sales

I am a sales representative for Florida Probe software, which enables hygienists to increase patient compliance with Scaling and Root Planning treatments. I specialize in transforming dental offices to empower hygienists, assistants, and dentists to work together as a cohesive team.
